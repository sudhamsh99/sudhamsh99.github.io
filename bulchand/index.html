A Legacy of Quality since 1933

A legacy of quality, Bulchand is a name associated with trust and quality. With humble beginnings in 1933, a small store of 600 Sft started by the visionary Mr. Bulchand Shamdas, at Abids road in the then Nizam state which is now Hyderabad, Telangana. We have always believed in the providing the best of quality merchandise to our valued clients. Bulchand & Co - Silk Palace, was established on the 1st of September 1933
