A Legacy of Quality since 1933

With humble beginnings in 1933, on September 1st, a small store of 600 Sft was started by the visionary Mr. Bulchand Shamdas at Abids road in the then Nizam state, which is now Hyderabad, Telangana. Known for our trust, quality, and experience in the trade, Bulchand & Co - Silk Palace always believed in providing the best quality merchandise to their valued clients. It has been successfully catering to customers' needs for over 80 years now.
