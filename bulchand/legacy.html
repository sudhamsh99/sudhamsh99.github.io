About Us

Bulchand is a name associated with trust and quality. With humble beginnings in 1933, a small store of 600 Sq.ft was started by the visionary Mr. Bulchand Shamdas at Abids road in the then Nizam state, which is now Hyderabad, Telangana. Bulchand & Co – Silk Palace was established on the 1st of September 1933. Beginning with fabrics and everything a departmental store could have, we have grown over the years and diversified into the market of weddings, selling the best of women’s clothes, home furnishings, and winter wear which we stock throughout the year for corporates, and people traveling to extreme climates. Fuelled with the thirst for growth, we have expanded our service of providing quality merchandise across shores to locations such as the West Indies. With four locations across the city, Bulchand is a one-stop shop in the best of fashion where you can shop for your home and you.
