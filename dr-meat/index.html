We monitor the feed and tell you all about it

The team at Dr. Meat works with recipes from nutritionists such that the birds feed on organic grains free of preservatives, hormones, steroids and antibiotics. And we tell you exactly what we feed them in keeping with the ideals of Transparent Farming when you order fresh chicken online.

The feed chosen ensures optimal nutrition for you with better meat: fat ratio as it includes maize, corn, soy, rice, and trace minerals that pass stringent quality tests. The maize we offer our birds is ethically sourced, and at times supplements like prebiotics and probiotics are provided when needed to ensure better health for the chicken. When you buy chicken online with us, we want you to know what the chicken eats because it affects your health too. So we monitor every step of the process to arm you with the information. We even adhere to WHO standards.

We choose the best water and process for consistent meat

We choose the best approach, and water is one of them. We provide the bird with the cleanest water that we can safely drink.

What sets us apart from other broiler meat e-retailers is that the water given to the birds is UV-purified, and it guarantees hygienic meat. Before online chicken delivery, the meat is cleaned and packed using specialized cooling techniques. The chilled water used in cleaning helps the meat retain moisture in the muscles making the meat juicier. Our meat is packed from the farms and delivered in 24 hours to you to retain freshness and ensure the birds go through the least amount of stress, making the meat you order online tender. Dr. Meat’s online chicken delivery happens across Bengaluru from farms close to the city. We make sure that the chicken is out of the farm and delivered to you within a few hours to keep it fresh.
